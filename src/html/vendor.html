About VMware Certifications

VMware is a technology company dealing with provision of cloud computing and virtualization software. VMware offers training and a number of credentials to validate the learnerâ€™s competency in working with VMware solutions. The certifications are categorized into six areas, namely, Data Center Virtualization, Network Virtualization, Cloud Management and Automation, Desktop and Mobility, Digital Business Transformation, and Digital Workspace.

The certifications fall into the following levels:

  • VMware Certified Design Expert (VCDX)
  • VMware Certified Advanced Professional (VCAP)
  • VMware Certified Professional (VCP)
  • VMware Certified Associate (VCA)

The Network Virtualization certifications and the exams details are:

  • Network Virtualization VCP6-NV
    Exam 2V0-642; 77 questions; duration: 120 minutes; cost: $250
  • Network Virtualization Deployment VCAP6-NV Deployment
    Exam 3V0-643; 23 questions; duration: 205 minutes; cost: $450
  • Network Virtualization Design Expert 6 VCDX60-NV

Here, a candidate chooses a design, and then has to defend it successfully before panellists.
